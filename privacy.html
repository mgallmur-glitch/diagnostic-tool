2. What Information We Collect / Qué Información Recolectamos

We collect the following information when you use our platform or purchase a subscription:

2.1 Information you provide directly:

  • • Full name and email address (when booking a diagnosis call or purchasing)
  • • Company name and role
  • • Phone number (optional, for onboarding)
  • • Payment information (processed by Lemon Squeezy — we never store card details)

2.2 Information collected automatically:

  • • IP address and browser type
  • • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • • UTM parameters and referral source

2.3 Business information (from clients):

  • • Sales call recordings provided voluntarily for AI analysis (Fase 2 — AI Closer Coach)
  • • CRM data from GoHighLevel (only what the client grants access to)
  • • Lead and contact information from client's pipeline

3. How We Use Your Information / Cómo Usamos Tu Información

We use collected information to:

  • • Deliver and operate the software services you subscribed to
  • • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • • Send service-related notifications (system updates, reports, alerts)
  • • Generate AI-powered call analysis reports for subscribed clients
  • • Improve the performance of our AI models and methodology
  • • Respond to support requests via Telegram or email
  • • Send weekly performance reports to subscribed clients

We do not:

  • • Sell your data to third parties
  • • Use your sales call data to train models for other clients
  • • Send unsolicited marketing emails without consent

5. Data Retention / Retención de Datos

  • • Client account data: Retained for the duration of the subscription + 90 days after cancellation
  • • Call recordings and transcriptions: Retained for 12 months, then permanently deleted unless the client requests earlier deletion
  • • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by standard accounting practices
  • • Website analytics: Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default)

Clients may request deletion of their data at any time by emailing hola@theclosingcodeai.com.

6. Your Rights / Tus Derechos

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • • Withdraw consent: At any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact: hola@theclosingcodeai.com

We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security / Seguridad de Datos

We implement the following security measures:

  • • All data transmitted over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption)
  • • Call recordings and transcriptions stored on encrypted VPS servers
  • • Client API keys stored with AES-256 encryption
  • • Access to client data restricted to the founding team only
  • • Payment data never stored on our servers (handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy)

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended exclusively for business professionals (B2B). We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify active clients via email.
